loopOutput

loopOutput["modelname",LBSM,fieldList]
calculates Wilson Coefficients generated by 1 loop level processes.

DetailsDetails

  • BSM Lagrangian and fieldList are taken as second and third argument respectively.
  • Use initializeLoop["modelname"] to define the matrix representations of respective the symmetries respected by the heavy multiplet in context. Use this "modelname" as the first argument of the 'loopOutput' function.
