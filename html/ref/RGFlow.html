CO D EX SYMBOL

RGFlow

RGFlow[{Operators,Wilson Coefficients},Matching Scale, μ]
Renormalization Group Flow of Wilson Coefficients of dimension six operators in Warsaw Basis from matching scale to arbitrary scale μ (μ < Matching Scale ).

DetailsDetails

  • Use output from 'treeOuput' and 'loopOutput' functions as first argument of 'RGFlow' function.
  • Put matching scale as the second argument.
  • μ can be any RG scale that is lower than the matching scale.
